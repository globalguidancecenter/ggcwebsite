FAQ

We've got the answers
you're looking for

What is GGC's mission?

+
GGC's mission is to transform the Quran from a book that Muslims respect into a living guide that they implement — One Ayah at a time. We are building the world's first comprehensive Quranic Guidance ecosystem, from curriculum to AI tools to physical books.

What does a GGC membership include?

+
Full access to the Quranic Guidance Curriculum (all levels, 3 languages), the complete Hidaya App with 200,000+ guidances, AI-powered guidance tools, all 6 GGC projects, unlimited Guided Circles, monthly live sessions with scholars, and all future programs at no extra cost.

Is GGC suitable for beginners?

+
Absolutely. Our curriculum is structured for all levels — from those who have never read the Quran, to reverts, to advanced students of Islamic knowledge. Everyone has a starting point and a clear path forward.

What languages is the curriculum available in?

+
The full Quranic Guidance Curriculum is available in Arabic, English, and Urdu — making it accessible to Muslims across the globe. More languages are in development.

How is GGC different from other Islamic education platforms?

+
GGC is the only platform focused specifically on the guidance dimension of the Quran — not just recitation or memorization, but practical life implementation. We combine a physical Mushaf, an AI app, live events, community circles, and structured curriculum under one coherent framework developed over 15 years.

Can I study at my own pace?

+
Yes. All curriculum content is available on-demand and self-paced. You can study whenever suits your schedule. Live Guided Circles and monthly scholar sessions are recorded and available to replay.

What is the Hidayah Mushaf?

+
The Hidayah Mushaf is a groundbreaking edition of the Quran that contains over 50,000 practical guidances — approximately 33 insights per page. It transforms every page of the Quran into an actionable life guide for Muslims and non-Muslims alike.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

+
Yes. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings with no penalties or fees.
